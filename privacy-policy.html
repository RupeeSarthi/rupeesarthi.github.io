Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. At Rupee Sarthi, we are committed to protecting the confidentiality and security of your personal information. We understand that your trust is our most valuable asset, and we are dedicated to maintaining the privacy of the information you share with us. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when using our website.

1. Information We Collect:

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and Contact information, including email address
  • Personal information, including date of birth and Permanent Account Number (PAN)
  • Demographic information such as gender and income
  • Other information that can help us improve our services

2. Why We Use Your Information:

We require this information for the following reasons:

  • To perform compliance checks and internal record keeping
  • To use the information to improve our products and services
  • To periodically send emails to your registered email address about new products, special offers, or other information which we think you may find interesting.

We do not share, sell, or lease your information to third parties, unless required by law or necessary for the services you avail. Occasionally, we may contact you for market research, but we won’t use your data for third-party marketing. Your trust is our priority, and we are committed to keeping your information secure.

3. Data Protection and Security:

At RupeeSarthi, your data security is paramount. We never share investor details with third parties unless explicitly mentioned in this policy or with your consent. We've put stringent security measures in place to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

We may enlist trusted third-party vendors for specific services, like document couriering and payment gateways, who are legally obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your information. Our commitment to data protection is unwavering, and we consistently enhance our security to ensure your data remains safe and secure with RupeeSarthi.

4. Understanding Our Cookie Usage:

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These small files help us analyze web traffic and customize our site to your preferences. We use this data solely for statistical analysis and improve our website accordingly. Cookies do not grant access to your computer or personal information unless voluntarily shared.

You can accept or decline cookies; however, disabling them might limit certain website features. You have control over cookies through your browser settings. Rest assured, we respect your privacy and use cookies solely to optimize your interaction with our site.

5. Links to Third-Party Websites:

While our website may feature links to other websites, please be aware that we have no control over the content and practices of these third-party sites. Once you leave our website using these links, we cannot be held responsible for the protection and privacy of any information you provide on those sites. Exercise caution and review the privacy policy applicable to the website you are visiting. Your security and privacy are important, and we encourage you to be vigilant when navigating external websites.

6.Legal Obligations:

In accordance with the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000, specifically under Section 43A, Prudent is mandated to uphold stringent security measures to protect your data. Moreover, as per Regulation 4 of the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, Prudent is obligated to furnish each registered user with an accessible copy of the information retained about you, ensuring full compliance with the law.

7. Updates to Privacy Policy:

Our Privacy Policy adheres to Regulation 4 of the Indian Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Information) Rules, 2011. We regularly review and update our policy to ensure compliance. Any changes made will be promptly posted on our website. We encourage you to revisit this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or the way your personal information is handled, please contact us at rupeesarthi@gmail.com.
